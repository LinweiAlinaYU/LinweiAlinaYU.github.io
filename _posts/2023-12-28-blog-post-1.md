During this summer vacation, I volunteered for 40 hours at the Chengdu Research Base of Giant Panda Breeding. My main responsibilities included conducting educational activities related to giant pandas, participating in wildlife conservation and environmental protection initiatives, and providing assistance in various related tasks.

<br/><img src='/images/PANDA-2.JPG' style='width: 48%;'> <img src='/images/PANDA-7.jpg' style='width: 51%;'>

Through my volunteer work, I had the opportunity to enhance my bilingual communication skills in both Chinese and English. I also acquired a substantial amount of specialized knowledge about giant pandas and their conservation. Additionally, I formed numerous new friendships. Utilizing my role as a volunteer, I also captured many adorable panda photos.
