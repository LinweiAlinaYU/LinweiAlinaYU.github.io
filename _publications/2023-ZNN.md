It is popular approach to employ zeroing neural networks (ZNN) to handle time-varying problems. However, most of the existing methods have limited flexibility in the discretization process, which seriously affects the final effect of the model. To solve this problem, in this paper, we present a new discrete-time ZNN (DTZNN) for handling discrete time-varying minimization problem. More details are shown in "Research" section.
